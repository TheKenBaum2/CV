Hobbies

I love playing video games and watching anime.
I also love using RPGMaker MZ to make games.

  • An Example of two games I made are 'Desert Walk' and 'Nexus'.

    • 'Desert walk' is a game where your spend weeks of IRL time walking through a massive desert while maintaining your hunger and thirst, made to be as boring and unfun as possible. This game has sense been deleted from my google drive for space reasons.

        Fun Fact: RPGMaker MZ uses a tile system for map making, with maps maxing out at 256x256 tiles in size, so in order to make the desert expansive enough, I made the map loop horizontally.
        I also set up a variable that counts each time the map has looped and set it so once the map has looped 9999 times, the game will cut to a 'You Win' Screen. To prevent players from cheating by just holding
        down the 'move right' button, the game also ask you to drink water every 8 screen loops and eat some food every 13 screen loops, in which you have to do as the game ask before the screen loops again,
        otherwise you got a game over. I also had a system where Cactus's were place randomly in the desert and would randomly change locations on each screen loop, this was to mask where each loop would happen.
        Also, is a player just so happened to walk into a cactus, that was game over as well. I made this game in less than 4 hours.

    • 'Nexus' was a simple game where you started in a hub area which had portals that led you to other one screen rooms, with the goal of the game being to find a key to open the big door in the nexus.
      The challenge of the game came from the obtuse puzzles I made and players figuring out which items to take from each rooms to solve puzzles in other rooms. This game was also deleted from google drive for space reasons.

        Fun Fact: I made this game in 19 hours, it was made with the intention of me making said game in less then 24 hours and I did it with time to spare.
